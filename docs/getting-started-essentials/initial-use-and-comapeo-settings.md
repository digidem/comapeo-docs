### Included Categories Set


Categories are the primary way people identify and related to information collected when mapping and monitoring. CoMapeo Mobile v8 includes an updated Categories Set that better represents the things being documented by communities and teams in areas where environmental and territorial threats are happening. Each category includes additional details that can be filled in to improve quality of observations


![](/images/initialuseandcomapeo_2.jpg)


> 💡 **Tip:** The easiest way is to explore the categories is in CoMapeo Mobile while creating new observations.


## CoMapeo Settings


### **<img src="/images/emojis/device_name_d1f67acffc237e7d_26522115.png" alt="app-icon-comapeo-device-name" className="emoji" style={{display: "inline", height: "1.2em", width: "auto", verticalAlign: "text-bottom", margin: "0 0.1em"}} />** **Device Name**


The only way to change a device name for use in CoMapeo is to use that same device. Device names are displayed in CoMapeo collaboration features like inviting teammates to join a project and viewing teammates in a project. 


### **<img src="/images/emojis/language_50ab50695da3c567_26522388.png" alt="app-icon-language" className="emoji" style={{display: "inline", height: "1.2em", width: "auto", verticalAlign: "text-bottom", margin: "0 0.1em"}} />** **Language**


Language options available may include languages with anywhere between 1% and 100% translations. 


### <img src="/images/emojis/coordinate_syst_7c6073e70a3daa2f_26522380.png" alt="app-icon-coordinate-system" className="emoji" style={{display: "inline", height: "1.2em", width: "auto", verticalAlign: "text-bottom", margin: "0 0.1em"}} /> **Coordinate System**


Choose one of the following options:


> 💡 **Tip:** Select the <img src="/images/emojis/coordinate_syst_7c6073e70a3daa2f_26522380.png" alt="app-icon-coordinate-system" className="emoji" style={{display: "inline", height: "1.2em", width: "auto", verticalAlign: "text-bottom", margin: "0 0.1em"}} /> Coordinate System option that is used most commonly in your context.


### <img src="/images/emojis/data_privacy_0b8b4afb48cdbb5c_26523100.png" alt="app-icon-data-privacy" className="emoji" style={{display: "inline", height: "1.2em", width: "auto", verticalAlign: "text-bottom", margin: "0 0.1em"}} /> **Data & Privacy**


Choose to **opt in** or **opt out** of sharing diagnostic data.


![](/images/initialuseandcomapeo_3.jpg)


Go to 🔗[**CoMapeo Data & Privacy**](https://digidem.notion.site/CoMapeo-Data-Privacy-d8f413bbbf374a2092655b89b9ceb2b0) to review the Privacy Policy in English
