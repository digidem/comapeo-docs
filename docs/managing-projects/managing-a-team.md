## What is a Team in CoMapeo


**Coordinators** can invite collaborators, manage project settings, and enable features like Remote Archive. 


**Participants** can contribute Observations and Tracks, and edit their own observations

# **Managing a Team**


The **Team** section allows you to see who is part of your current project and how devices identify themselves. This helps team members coordinate work, understand who collected specific observations, and make sure devices are labeled correctly.


This page explains how to view your project’s team members, update your device name, and understand how names appear in observations.


---


## **Accessing the Team Page**

1. Open the :app-icon-menu: **Menu** from the main navigation.
2. Tap **Team**.

This opens the list of all devices participating in your project.

## **Understanding the Team List**


The Team page displays every device that has joined the current project. Each entry may show:

- **Device name** (how the user identified their device)
- **Last active time** or connection indicator (if supported)
- **Profile icon or initials** (project dependent)

This list helps you confirm who is collecting data and how devices appear in shared observations.

## **Device Names and Their Purpose**


Your device name is used to:

- Identify who recorded each observation
- Help team members collaborate
- Distinguish devices in a shared project
- Support local sync workflows (when enabled)

Choosing a clear, recognizable name helps avoid confusion in the field.


---


## **Editing Your Device Name**


To update your device’s name:

1. Go to the **Team** page
2. Tap :app-icon-edit: **Edit name** (or the current device entry)
3. Enter a new name
4. Tap :app-icon-save-observation: **Save**

Your updated device name will appear in future observations you create.

## **How Team Information Appears in Observations**


When viewing an observation, you may see:

- **Recorded by:** followed by the device name
- **Device ID** (if shown by your project)

## **Removing Team Members**


In most projects, team membership is automatic based on devices that have joined the project. Removing a device from the team list is typically not available directly inside the app. Team membership resets when:

- The project is reset
- A device leaves and rejoins a different project

If removal or security-related access control is needed, consult your project administrator.

## **Tips & Good Practices**


:::note 💡
<callout icon="💡" color="gray_bg"> Choose short, descriptive device names, such as “Maria GPS 1” or “Patrol Team B”. </callout>


💡 <callout icon="💡" color="gray_bg"> If multiple people use the same device, update the device name when users change to avoid confusion. </callout>


⚠️ <callout icon="⚠️" color="gray_bg"> Device names appear in exported or shared data. Avoid personal or sensitive information when naming your device. </callout>
:::
