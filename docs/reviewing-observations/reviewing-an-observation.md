After an observation has been saved, you can open it from the map or from the observation list to review all the information that was recorded. Reviewing an observation helps confirm details, check evidence, and ensure the data is complete and accurate.


When you open an observation, several sections appear:

- **Details**
- **GPS Information**
- **Photo Information**
- **Edit Options**

Each section is explained below.


---


## **Details**


The **Details** section shows the main information saved when the observation was created. This includes:

- Category
- Description or notes
- Answers from the **Details** form (if completed)
- Any text fields or structured questions associated with the chosen category
- Date and time the observation was saved

Use this section to confirm that the information correctly describes what was observed. If anything needs to be added or clarified, you can update it using the **Edit** tool.


---


## **GPS Information**


Every saved observation includes GPS coordinates collected at the moment it was saved. In this section you will see:

- Latitude and longitude
- GPS accuracy (when available)
- Additional metadata provided by the device

This information helps validate where the observation was taken.


If coordinates were entered manually, or if accuracy was low at the moment of saving, this section will still display the values that were saved.


GPS information **cannot be edited** after saving.


---


## **Photo Information**


Any photos taken while creating the observation appear here. For each photo, you may see:

- The image
- Date and time it was captured
- Photo metadata (including GPS, when available)

Photos provide visual confirmation and strengthen evidence.


Photos **cannot be deleted after the observation has been saved**, so it is important to remove any unwanted images **before saving** during the creation process.


Tap on a photo to view it in full screen and to see the metadata that was captured.


---


## **Editing an Observation**


Some parts of an observation can be edited after saving. This is helpful when you need to add more detail, correct a description, or complete unanswered fields.


### Editable information includes:

- Description or notes
- Details/questions associated with the category
- Additional photos
- Additional audio recordings

### Information that **cannot** be edited:

- GPS coordinates
- Date and time
- Photos that were already saved

To edit an observation, open it and tap the <img src="/comapeo-docs/images/emojis/edit_1e513871ec012d90_91535411.png" alt="app-icon-edit" className="emoji" style={{display: "inline", height: "1.2em", width: "auto", verticalAlign: "text-bottom", margin: "0 0.1em"}} /> **Edit** button. This will open the editor where changes can be made.


After editing, tap <img src="/comapeo-docs/images/emojis/save_observatio_638bd525ec54c819_91535197.png" alt="app-icon-save-observation" className="emoji" style={{display: "inline", height: "1.2em", width: "auto", verticalAlign: "text-bottom", margin: "0 0.1em"}} /> **Save** to update the observation.


---


## **Tips and Good Practices**


> 💡 **Tip**: Use reviewing time to check if notes are clear, photos are usable, and required details are completed. This improves data quality and makes observations more useful for your project.


> ⚠️ **Warning:** Location information (GPS coordinates) and photos saved at the moment of creation cannot be changed later. Make sure they are correct before saving a new observation.
