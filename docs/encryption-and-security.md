# Encryption & Security


Data is encrypted in transit


:::note ⚠️ Warning
Indexed data is not encrypted at rest. there are plans to do this in the future
:::
The [**Personal and physical safety guide for Earth Defenders**](https://www.earthdefenderstoolkit.com/toolkit/guide-personal-and-physical-safety-for-earth-defenders/) aims to support Earth Defenders to identify and address direct risks such as violence and criminalization.


[**The Digital Security for Earth Defenders Guide**](https://www.earthdefenderstoolkit.com/toolkit/guide-digital-security-for-earth-defenders/) aims to help identify digital risks and think through countermeasures and contingency plans


> 💡 **Tip:** Consider primary security measures and taking time to agree on use of privacy and security features of CoMapeo
