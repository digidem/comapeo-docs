## What Data Sharing & Privacy


**Data Sharing** is a passive function of CoMapeo that works when a device is connected to the internet. Most apps use this function to gain information about app use to improve functionality. Some apps use data sharing for other purposes including surveillance, targeted advertising  and profit. 


**Data privacy** is the way in which an app, and platform host will protect your privacy. This this may include anonymization or storage maintenance procedures.


> 💡 **Tip**: It is always recommended to be familiar with data sharing and privacy policies for any app you download


### CoMapeo Data Privacy

- **Private by default.** The choice to opt in is offered during onboarding
- **No personally identifiable information** is gathered at any time
- **You’re in control.** Change the setting any time

Review CoMapeo Privacy Policy to clarify doubts at any time. Choose to **opt in** or **opt out** of sharing diagnostic data.

# **Adjusting Data Sharing & Privacy**


The **Data Sharing & Privacy** settings allow you to choose how your observations, tracks, and device information are shared with others. These settings give you more control over what you share inside a project, especially when working with sensitive or personal data.


This page explains how to access privacy settings, what each option means, and how changing them affects your project.


---


## **Accessing Data Sharing & Privacy Settings**

1. Open the :app-icon-menu: **Menu**
2. Tap **Settings**
3. Select **Data Sharing & Privacy**

## **Available Privacy Settings**


Depending on your project configuration, you may see some or all of the following privacy options:


### **1. Share My Observations**


Allows your observations and tracks to be visible to other devices in the same project.

- **Enabled:** Other team members can see your data
- **Disabled:** Only your device can view your own data

Controls whether your device name appears on observations.

- **Enabled:** Observations show who recorded them

### **3. Allow Observation Sync**


If your project supports local data sharing between devices (such as over Wi-Fi or Bluetooth), this toggle controls whether your data participates in sync operations.

- **Enabled:** Your device shares and receives new observations
- **Disabled:** Your device keeps its data private

## **Understanding How Privacy Affects Your Work**


When privacy settings are adjusted:

- Other team members may see more or less of your content
- Your device may stop participating in data sync
- Observations you record may appear anonymous
- Photos may remain only on your device

These changes do **not** delete data—only control how it is shared.


---


## **Temporary vs. Permanent Settings**


You can adjust privacy settings at any time.


Examples:

- Turn off sharing during sensitive work
- Turn sharing back on for team-based monitoring
- Hide your device name temporarily
- Pause data syncing during training sessions or demos

Changes apply immediately and do not require restarting the app.


---


## **Resetting Privacy Settings**


To return all privacy settings to default:

1. Go to **Settings → Data Sharing & Privacy**
2. Tap **Reset to Default Settings** (if available)

Your project may define its own default privacy rules.
